HOW IT WORKS

1

CAPTURE OR DRAW

Take a photo of your metal part next to a reference object (ruler, credit card, coin) — or switch to Draw Mode and tap/drag your shape directly on screen.

2

CALIBRATE SCALE

Tell the app the real-world size of your reference object. The CV engine computes an exact pixels-per-inch constant for your shot.

3

CONTOUR DETECTION

OpenCV Canny edge detection finds the part outline, the RDP algorithm simplifies it into straight-line segments, and every segment is labeled and measured.

4

3D REVIEW

Finger-drag the 3D viewer to orbit your extruded part from any angle. Dimension callouts float on every edge. Tap any piece to assign a weld symbol.

5

ADJUST PARAMETERS

Dial in metal thickness, kerf type, wire diameter, and fillet leg size. Welder calculations update instantly.

6

EXPORT

Dimensions are always free. Pay $2.99 for a print-ready PDF blueprint or $9.99 for a CNC-ready DXF file. One-time charge, no subscription.
